Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below you will add a specific amount after clicking on them to your total score. You win the game after you match the score to random number, or loose if score is greater than number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
